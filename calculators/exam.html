Frequently Asked Questions

GPA (Grade Point Average): Average of grade points earned in a single semester or term.

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average): Average of grade points earned across all semesters/terms of an academic program.

Simply put, GPA is for a specific period, while CGPA is the cumulative average of all periods.

  • 4-point scale: 3.5+ (Excellent), 3.0-3.4 (Very Good), 2.5-2.9 (Good)

For most competitive programs and jobs, a CGPA above 8.0 (10-point) or 3.5 (4-point) is considered excellent.
